title: Laguna WWTP
---
I was a design engineer on a wastewater treatment plant
improvement project for the City of Santa Rosa. The 17.5 million
gallon average day plant required a full civil site improvement
as well as treatment process improvements. A site plan and civil
and mechanical details were created using AutoCAD civil 3D. The
scope also included an engineer’s cost estimate for the 15
million dollar project.
