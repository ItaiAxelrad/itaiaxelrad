As a project engineer for the Baseline team, I was responsible
for many improvement projects at the 8 MGD Sid Copeland Water
Treatment Plant in the City of Louisville, Colorado. Tasks
included updating phased out infrastructure, finding innovative
retrofitting solutions, and keeping up with the ever-changing
regulatory agencies. The designs focused on long term
future-proof solutions and value engineering.
