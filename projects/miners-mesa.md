I was part of the Baseline team that designed over 7,800 ft of sanitary sewer main to service a substantial phased new development as well as existing facilities on Miners Mesa in the City of Black Hawk. The size, alignment, and profile of the proposed sanitary sewer main was evaluated and designed to meet Black Hawk Central City Sanitation District (BHCCSD) and City of Black Hawk’s standards. The hydraulic design was evaluated using Bentley’s SewerCAD software to ensure a functional, efficient and cost effective design. Repaving of the roadway above the sanitary main was incorporated into the design as well. The system will be owned and maintained by the BHCCSD and funded by a public-private partnership.
